{# Repair tools #}


    
Data Repair

    

        Recompute every thread's last_poster_id/name/avatar from the posts table
        (the actual MAX(post_date) per thread). Use this to fix stale "last poster"
        data caused by older crawler bugs. Safe — only updates threads where the
        computed value differs from what's stored. Avatars get cleared so they
        re-resolve from the corrected character.
    

    
    


    

    

        Delete threads (and their posts/quotes/links) belonging to any forum
        in forums_excluded. Use this after adding a new OOC forum
        to the exclusion list to clean up legacy data.
    

    
    




{# Purge & re-crawl #}


    
Purge & Re-crawl

    

        Wipes all topics, quotes, and post data, then triggers a full re-crawl.
        Characters and profile fields are kept. This cannot be undone.
    

    
    

    

    
Full Nuke

    

        Wipes everything — characters, profiles, topics, quotes, posts.
        Re-discovers all characters from scratch. You will lose all data.
